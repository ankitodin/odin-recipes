Butter Chicken

Hello Guys, Today we are making North Indias famous Butter Chicken

List of Ingredients

  1. 2 teaspoons garam masala
  2. 2 teaspoons tandooori masala powder
  3. 2 teaspoons Madras Curry Powder
  4. 1 teaspoon ground cummin
  5. ½ teaspoon ground cardamom
  6. ½ teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  7. salt and ground black pepper to taste
  8. 1 ½ pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-size pieces
  9. 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  10. 1 yellow onion, chopped
  11. 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  12. 4 cloves garlic, minced
  13. 2 teaspoons chopped fresh ginger
  14. 1 cup tomato puree
  15. 1 cup half-and-half
  16. ¼ cup plain yogurt
  17. ⅓ cup cashews
  18. 1 bunch fresh cilantro, stems removed
